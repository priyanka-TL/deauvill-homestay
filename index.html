Our Story & Philosophy

Deauvill Homestay is more than just a place to stay; it's a heartfelt endeavor to share the warmth and authenticity of Kerala with travelers from around the globe. Established in 2008 by NG Pradeep, our homestay was born from a passion for genuine hospitality and a deep love for Varkala's serene beauty.

Our philosophy is simple: to create a home-away-from-home experience where every guest feels cherished and connected to the local culture. We primarily offer comfortable stays, and for our valued long-term guests, we provide access to a shared kitchen facility. We believe in personalized service, attending to the unique needs of each visitor, and offering genuine insights into the vibrant life of Varkala. We strive to make your stay truly memorable and enriching

Our Idyllic Location: Varkala, Kerala

Varkala, a jewel on the Arabian Sea coast, is a serene coastal town in the southern part of Kerala, India. It's globally renowned for its distinctive cliff formations, pristine golden beaches, and natural mineral springs believed to have medicinal properties. It's a magnet for spiritual seekers, beach enthusiasts, and those seeking rejuvenating Ayurvedic treatments and tranquil yoga retreats.

Deauvill Homestay boasts a truly prime location. We are just a 2-minute walk from the beautiful Varkala beach**, offering unparalleled convenience for your seaside adventures. Furthermore, our homestay is within easy walking distance of supermarkets, various shops, and a wide array of restaurants, ensuring all your daily needs and culinary desires are met effortlessly. The Varkala Railway Station is also conveniently located just 3.5 km away.

Our Commitment to You

We are deeply committed to fostering sustainable tourism practices, actively supporting local communities, and diligently preserving the breathtaking natural beauty of Varkala. Every aspect of our homestay is designed with environmental consciousness and community well-being in mind.
